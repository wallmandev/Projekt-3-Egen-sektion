Africa by Origin

In a time where humanity's impact seems ubiquitous, it's becoming more and more difficult to experience Earth in its most primal form. That feeling of untouched wilderness teeming with wildlife can still be found however on a safari in Africa. Venture off to the plains of the Serengeti in Tanzania to see the Great Migration of wildebeest. Navigate the lush jungles of Congo as you track the elusive gorilla. Hop in a boat as you float through the flooded Okavango Delta of Botswana as you witness the complex interactions of predator and prey. There is an abundance of wildlife experiences to undertake and it all can be complemented by the beautiful interactions with the people of this stunning continent.
